Watercolors

1. The first step is to do a sketch of your final idea for then eraser it a little bit of course the papers must be for watercolors or a thick paper.

2. The texture of the paper must be soft, you choose how soften you want.

3. The materials you need are watercolors, brushes, two cups of water and napkins.

4. You need have consider when you use more watercolor the color will intense and when you use more water the color will clear (this can use if you want a light color).

5. Use black and white for have dark or light colors is a mistake so please don’t do that.

6. If you want dark color use a complementary color or a color can match. For example, if you want a dark red you can mix red and green (complementary colors) or mix red and blue (colors that match).

7. So if you want specific the better is first study color theory.

8. If you want lights in your painting or drawing, you can use the paper doing spaces in blank but other ways are use wite out, gouache, acrylic paint, etc.

9. You can use the napkins if you had mistake in choose a color.
