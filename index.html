What can you learn in this page?

In this page you are going to learn about techniques of art and some tips of art. The majority of the tips are for beginners, but some can be interesting for a person who have a lot of time in the drawing.

Sketch

In Sketch section you are going to learn how to do sketches, some exercises for be easy and things that you don’t have do to.

Colored pencils

In Colored Pencils section you are going to learn how paint a surface, what is the better way for paint and other ways of use the colored pencils.

Watercolors

In Watercolors section you are going to learn which are the mistakes of the watercolors, how control the water and what paper is better for the watercolors.

Theory of Color

In Theory of Color section you are going to watch my video to learn about the primary colors, secondary colors, complementary colors and which colors can match together.
